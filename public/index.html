The Problem
Grocery apps are built like e-commerce stores. Shoppers think in plain language — "I need stuff for tacos, we're out of milk" — but every major app forces browse → search → click across hundreds of SKUs. Mobile cart abandonment hits 83%. The interface hasn't caught up to how people actually think.
The Solution: Speech-To-Checkout
InstaKart is a speech-first grocery ordering experience. Just say what you need — AI interprets it, maps it to live inventory, and builds your cart.
🎤 Speech-First — No browsing. No taps. No filters.
🤖 AI Cart Building — Natural language → real products & prices
💡 Transparent AI — Surfaces its assumptions as it builds
Speech-To-Checkout isn't a feature. It's a better starting point.
